“Welcome to Times Square!”

The guide yells coarsely into a miniature megaphone, competing with the cacophony of sales pitches from Broadway ticket sellers,
touts from the mascot mafia (especially from a Catwoman impersonator), and the blare from the old skool boomboxes from various street performers.

She is failing miserably.

This is your first day in New York, and your tour agency has swiftly whisked you to the middle of Times Square.

Camera in hand, you wait expectantly for a magical moment to appear. Maybe a celebrity sighting,
a spontaneous performance by the Broadway cast of Lion King, a rat dragging a pizza across town...

Yawn...
